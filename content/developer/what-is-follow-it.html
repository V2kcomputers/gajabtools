What is Follow.it?

Follow.it is an online content subscription platform that allows website owners and bloggers to automatically send their latest posts to subscribers via email notifications. It works using RSS feeds and helps increase traffic by bringing users back whenever new content is published.

{{}}

How Follow.it Works

  • Connect your website RSS feed (example: /index.xml)
  • Users subscribe using email or follow button
  • New posts are automatically detected
  • Subscribers receive email notifications instantly

Key Features

  • Automatic email notifications
  • RSS feed integration
  • Simple setup (no coding required)
  • Subscriber management system
  • Analytics and tracking

Advantages

  • Beginner-friendly setup
  • Free plan available
  • Fully automated system
  • Increases returning visitors
  • No manual email sending required

Disadvantages

  • Free plan includes branding
  • Limited email design customization
  • Advanced features are paid

How to Use Follow.it

  1. Create an account on Follow.it
  2. Add your website RSS feed
  3. Customize subscription form
  4. Embed the form on your website
  5. Start collecting subscribers

Who Should Use Follow.it?

  • Bloggers and website owners
  • News and update websites
  • Content creators
  • Anyone who wants automated email updates

Best Alternatives to Follow.it

1. Mailchimp

Mailchimp is a professional email marketing platform that provides advanced automation, email templates, and full branding control.

  • Advanced automation workflows
  • Custom email design
  • High deliverability
  • Free plan available (limited)

2. ConvertKit

ConvertKit is designed for creators and bloggers. It offers simple automation, tagging system, and monetization features like paid newsletters.

  • Easy to use interface
  • Automation with tagging system
  • Best for creators
  • Paid features required for advanced tools

3. MailerLite

MailerLite is a budget-friendly alternative with powerful features like drag-and-drop editor, automation, and subscriber management.

  • Affordable pricing
  • Automation available
  • Modern email editor
  • Free plan with good limits

4. Substack

Substack is a simple newsletter platform that combines blogging and email subscription in one place.

  • Very easy setup
  • Blog + email in one platform
  • Paid subscription option
  • Limited customization

Conclusion

Follow.it is a great beginner-friendly tool for automatic email notifications using RSS feeds. However, if you want more control, customization, and advanced features, tools like Mailchimp, ConvertKit, or MailerLite can be better options. The best choice depends on your needs, budget, and long-term goals.
