NotebookLM by Google – Complete Guide

NotebookLM is an advanced AI-powered note-taking and research tool developed by Google. It helps users understand documents, summarize content, and generate insights from uploaded materials like PDFs, Google Docs, and text files.

Note: NotebookLM was initially introduced as "Project Tailwind" during its experimental phase.
