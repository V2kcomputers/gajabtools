🤖 What is Gemini AI?

Gemini AI is an advanced Artificial Intelligence system developed by Google. It is a powerful multimodal AI tool that can understand and work with text, images, audio, video, and documents.

Gemini works as a Smart Chatbot + Digital Assistant + Creative Tool — all in one platform.
