Pixabay

Free Stock Images, Videos & Music Platform

What is Pixabay?

Pixabay is a free stock media platform that provides high-quality images, videos, illustrations, vectors, and music. All content is free to download and can be used for both personal and commercial purposes.

{{}}

Key Features of Pixabay

  • Completely free to use
  • No attribution required
  • High-quality HD and 4K media
  • Large collection of photos, videos, and music
  • Easy search and filtering options

Types of Content Available

  • Photos
  • Videos
  • Illustrations
  • Vectors
  • Music & Sound Effects

Pixabay License

Most content on Pixabay is released under the Pixabay License, which allows you to:

  • Use content for free
  • Modify and edit images
  • Use for commercial purposes

Note: Avoid using content for illegal, misleading, or brand misuse purposes.

How to Use Pixabay

  1. Visit the Pixabay website
  2. Search for your desired content
  3. Choose the image or video
  4. Download in your preferred size
  5. Use it in your project

Where You Can Use Pixabay Content

  • Websites and blogs
  • YouTube videos
  • Social media posts
  • Graphic design projects

Advantages

  • No cost involved
  • No copyright issues
  • Huge library of content

Limitations

  • Some content may require careful use (people/brands)
  • Sponsored images may appear

Conclusion

Pixabay is a powerful and reliable platform for finding free stock media. Whether you are a developer, designer, or content creator, it helps you create professional content without spending money.
