Edits: Video Editor

Complete Guide for Beginners & Creators

📱 What is Edits: Video Editor?

Edits: Video Editor is a powerful video editing application developed by Instagram (Meta). It is designed for content creators who want to create professional-quality videos for Reels, Shorts, and social media.

🔥 Key Features

  • Advanced timeline video editing
  • AI-powered background remover
  • Auto caption generation
  • Professional filters and effects
  • Royalty-free music library
  • 4K video export support

🚀 How to Use

  • Download and install the app from Play Store
  • Open the app and select your video
  • Use timeline to cut, trim, and edit clips
  • Add effects, filters, music, and captions
  • Preview your video
  • Export in high quality and share
