Zoho Forms - Complete Guide

📌 What is Zoho Forms?

Zoho Forms is an online form builder tool that allows users to create forms without coding. You can create registration forms, surveys, contact forms, and collect data easily.

✔ No coding required
✔ Easy drag-and-drop builder
✔ Works on mobile and desktop

🔧 Uses of Zoho Forms

1. Registration & Application

  • School/College admission forms
  • Event registration
  • Job application forms

2. Feedback & Survey

  • Customer feedback
  • Website survey
  • Service reviews

3. Business Use

  • Lead generation
  • Client inquiry forms
  • Order & payment collection

4. Data Collection

  • Export to Excel or Google Sheets
  • Email notifications
  • Workflow automation

👨‍💻 Who Can Use Zoho Forms?

  • Students: Projects, surveys, quizzes
  • Website Owners: Contact & lead forms
  • Business Owners: Customer data & orders
  • Freelancers: Client forms & bookings
  • Government / Schemes: Application forms
👉 Zoho Forms is NOT only for office work. It is useful for everyone.

  • Easy drag-and-drop builder
