DMCA Policy

Effective Date: 19 February 2026

Website: https://gajabtools.com

GajabTools.com respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects users of our website to do the same.

If you believe that any content available on our website infringes your copyright, you may submit a DMCA takedown request.

1. Copyright Infringement Notification

If you are a copyright owner (or authorized to act on behalf of one) and believe that your copyrighted material has been used on our website without permission, please provide the following information:

  • Your full name and contact information (email address required)
  • A description of the copyrighted work
  • The exact URL or location of the allegedly infringing content
  • A statement that you believe in good faith that the use is not authorized
  • A statement that the information in your notice is accurate
  • Your electronic or physical signature

2. How to Submit a DMCA Notice

Please send your DMCA complaint to:

  • Email: contact@gajabtools.com
  • Subject Line: DMCA Complaint

3. Review and Action

Upon receiving a valid DMCA complaint:

  • We will review the request promptly.
  • If necessary, we will remove or disable access to the allegedly infringing content.
  • We may notify the user responsible for the content.

4. Counter Notification

If you believe that content removed due to a DMCA notice was removed by mistake, you may submit a counter-notification including:

  • Your name and contact details
  • Identification of the removed content
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you believe removal was a mistake
  • Your electronic or physical signature

5. Repeat Infringers

We reserve the right to restrict or terminate access to users who repeatedly infringe intellectual property rights.

6. Client-Side Tools Disclaimer

GajabTools.com primarily provides browser-based tools that operate entirely on the user's device.

  • We do not store user-uploaded files on our servers.
  • We do not maintain a database of user content.

Therefore, any copyright issues typically relate only to publicly visible website content.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this DMCA Policy at any time. Updates will be reflected on this page.

GajabTools.com respects copyright law and takes infringement claims seriously.
