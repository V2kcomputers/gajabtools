Cookie Policy

Effective Date: 19 February 2026

Website: https://gajabtools.com

This Cookie Policy explains how GajabTools.com uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

GajabTools.com is a privacy-focused, browser-based tools platform. We do not store user data on our servers.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve user experience and website functionality.

2. How We Use Cookies

We may use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Website functionality and performance
  • Analytics (such as Google Analytics)
  • Advertising (such as Google AdSense)
  • Remembering user preferences (if applicable)

3. Client-Side Tools & Local Storage

All tools on GajabTools.com operate entirely within your browser.

  • Any data entered into tools is processed locally.
  • Some tools may use browser LocalStorage to temporarily store settings.
  • No files or personal data are uploaded to our servers.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies, including:

  • Google Analytics (traffic analysis)
  • Google AdSense (advertising)

These third-party services have their own privacy and cookie policies.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Please note that disabling cookies may affect some website features.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Cookie Policy, you may contact us:

  • Email: contact@gajabtools.com
  • Website: https://gajabtools.com
By continuing to use GajabTools.com, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.
