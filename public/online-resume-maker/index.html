Resume Generator – Required Information Details

A Resume is one of the most important documents for job applications. It provides a summary of your personal details, educational qualifications, skills, work experience, and achievements. The Resume Generator Tool helps you create a professional Resume, CV, or Biodata within minutes.

✨ Create Professional Resume

How to make resume : The table below explains each field required in the Resume Generator and what information you should enter.

Field Name Description Example
Candidate Name Enter your full name as mentioned in your official documents. Vikram Singh
Candidate Address Enter your current or permanent residential address. Huzurganj, Rewa, Madhya Pradesh, India
Mobile Number Provide an active mobile number for communication. 9876543210
Email Address Enter a professional and valid email address. vikram@example.com
Date of Birth Enter your date of birth in the required format. 15-08-2000
Father's Name Provide your father's full name. Rajendra Singh
Mother's Name Provide your mother's full name. Sushila Devi
Gender Select your gender. Male / Female / Other
Nationality Enter your nationality. Indian
Marital Status Select your marital status. Married / Unmarried
Languages Known List the languages you can read, write, or speak. English, Hindi
Hobbies Mention your hobbies and interests. Reading, Traveling, Cricket
Career Objective Write a short statement describing your career goals and professional aspirations. To work in a challenging environment where I can utilize my skills and contribute to organizational growth.
Academic Qualification Add details of your educational background. 10th, 12th, Bachelor's Degree
Board / University Enter the name of the board, college, or university. CBSE, MP Board, APS University
Passing Year Specify the year in which you completed the qualification. 2024
Marks / Percentage Enter your obtained marks, percentage, or CGPA. 82%
Division / Grade Enter the division or grade achieved. First Division
Professional Qualification Add technical, vocational, or professional courses completed. DCA, PGDCA, ITI, Diploma
Extra Qualification Mention additional certifications, training programs, or special skills. CCC, Tally, Typing Certificate
Work Experience Describe your previous job roles, responsibilities, and duration of employment. Computer Operator – 2 Years
Photo Upload Upload a recent passport-size photograph. Professional Passport Photo
